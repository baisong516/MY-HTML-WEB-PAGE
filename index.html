henan university

is a University jointly established by the Henan provincial government and the ministry of education of the People's Republic of China.World first-class discipline construction in colleges and universities, "111" plan, the Midwest university foundation ability construction, excellent doctor education training plan, outstanding legal talent education training program planning, outstanding teachers, the national college students' innovative entrepreneurial training plan [1], the national colleges and universities to deepen reform of creative education demonstration [2] [3], the national college students' cultural quality education base, the Chinese government scholarship students receiving institutions in China, etc. In 1912, Mr. Lin boxiang and other educators from henan province established henan preparatory school for studying abroad in Europe and America (predecessor of heda) on the site of gongyuan, kaifeng state in the qing dynasty [4].In 1927, general feng yuxiang rebuilt it into the fifth national sun yat-sen university, which went through the stages of zhongzhou university, the fifth national sun yat-sen university, the provincial henan university, the national henan university, and henan normal university.In 1984, the name of henan university was restored.





The important course of history






  • 1902 henan university was founded in kaiying gate, kaifeng city, henan province.
  • On March 3, 1923, based on the foundation of henan preparatory school for studying abroad in Europe and America, zhongzhou university was rebuilt and the opening ceremony was held
  • 1944, heda was ranked the third national university in China by the ministry of education.
  • In 1949, the left wing of heda went to wuhan and the right wing went to Taiwan.
  • In May 1984, it was restored to henan university, which was gradually transformed from a normal university into a comprehensive university.
  • In July 2004, comrade jiang zemin wrote the title of "advancing with The Times, pioneering and innovating, and making henan university a national first-class university"
  • In December 2015, six disciplines of henan university were shortlisted into the "advantageous characteristic discipline plan" with a heavy investment of 3.1 billion yuan in henan province
  • In September 2017, it was selected into the first batch of national "" double first-class" "world-class discipline construction universities
